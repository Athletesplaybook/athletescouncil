The Council Team Leaders

James Donaldson,

CEO

Over a 25-year career, John Stone has been a leader in educational reform, sports media and marketing. Early in his career, John taught middle school social studies in some of Houston’s most challenging schools working as a member of Teach for America. Following a master’s degree at Columbia Teachers College, John earned a joint MBA/MS in business and communications that led him to leadership positions in marketing and media over the past 15 years. \u2028 In 2003, John won an Emmy Award for his work on the HBO documentary, \u2028Curse of the Bambino. He has also been a key member of teams launching new programming at ESPN and new networks such as the Fox Business Network in 2007. As Director of New Business Development for the New England Consulting Group in 2010, he led the firm into new sectors like education, entertainment \u2028and sports.
